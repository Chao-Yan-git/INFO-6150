Click the name or picture to chooce what you like:

  • Espresso

    a finely ground very dark roast coffee, packed somewhat tightly in a special metal filter, with water "expressed" through it under pressure.

  • Cappuccino

    A 1:1:1 ratio of espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam.

  • Macchiato

    Typically in North America, this is a cappuccino, but with the steamed milk component missing.

  • Latte

    A "wet" cappuccino. Hold back the foam with a spoon, and pour the warm milk over the espresso. Put a little bit of foam on top for decoration only.
